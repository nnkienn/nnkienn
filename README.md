## 🧠 About Me
I'm a graduate from the **University of Science (VNUHCM – HCMUS)**, majoring in **Software Technology**.  

I am a **Software Engineer** operating at the intersection of **Backend Architecture** and **Applied Artificial Intelligence**. My strength lies in not just building AI features, but engineering the robust infrastructure required to run them at scale in production.

* **On the Backend:** I design and develop microservice-based architectures using **Java (Spring Boot)** and **Python (FastAPI)**, managing real-time data flows with **RabbitMQ** and **Redis**.
* **On the AI Side:** I specialize in **Applied AI**, building Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines, intelligent agents, and **integrating LLMs into enterprise systems (ERP) to automate complex business workflows and data processing**.

My ultimate goal is to bridge the gap between heavy-duty backend systems and cutting-edge AI technologies to deliver scalable, intelligent applications.
